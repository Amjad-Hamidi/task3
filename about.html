Abu Altheeb Restaurant

Abu Altheeb Restaurant, located in the Southern District of Al-Montazah Street near Al-Asma'i School in Tulkarm, West Bank, Palestine, offers a diverse menu featuring a variety of delicious foods. Patrons can enjoy shawarma in plates, wraps, or baguettes, as well as crispy chicken, Zinger Burgers, French fries, and a selection of appetizers. The restaurant also provides a range of beverages, including water, soft drinks, and energy drinks.
